Priority Preference

[Observation: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143]

This template represents priority preferences chosen by a patient or a care provider. Priority preferences are choices made by care providers or patients or both relative to options for care or treatment (including scheduling, care experience, and meeting of personal health goals), the sharing and disclosure of health information, and the prioritization of concerns and problems.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-30951, CONF:1098-30952 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143"
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="OBS" Observation (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 HL7ActClass) (CONF:1098-30949)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode="EVN" Event (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1001 HL7ActMood) (CONF:1098-30950)
  4. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF:1098-30953)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1098-30954)/@code="225773000" Preference (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.96 SNOMEDCT) (CONF:1098-30955, CONF:1098-30956)
  6. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-32327)
  7. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] value with @xsi:type="CD", which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Priority Level 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.60 STATIC 2014-06-11 (CONF:1098-30957)
  8. SHOULD contain [0..*] author such that it (CONF:1098-30958)
    1. Conforms to Author Participation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119)
