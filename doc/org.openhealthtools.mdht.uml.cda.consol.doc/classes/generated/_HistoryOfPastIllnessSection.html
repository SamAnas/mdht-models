History Of Past Illness Section - conformance rules

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.20]

Known Subclasses: History of Past Illness Section (V3)

This section contains a record of the patient's past complaints, problems, and diagnoses. It contains data from the patient's past up to the patient's current complaint or reason for seeking medical care.

  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] title (CONF:7830, R2.1=CONF:1198-7830)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] text (CONF:7831, R2.1=CONF:1198-7831)
