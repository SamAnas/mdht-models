Medication Dispense2 - conformance rules

[Supply: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.18]

This template records the act of supplying medications (i.e., dispensing).

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-7453, CONF:1098-10505, CONF:1098-32580 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.18"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09"
  2. SHALL conform to Medication Dispense template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.18)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-7455), which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Medication Fill Status 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.12.80.64 STATIC 2014-04-23 (CONF:1098-32361)
  4. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] repeatNumber (CONF:1098-7457)

    In "INT" (intent) mood, the repeatNumber defines the number of allowed administrations. For example, a repeatNumber of "3" means that the substance can be administered up to 3 times. In "EVN" (event) mood, the repeatNumber is the number of occurrences. For example, a repeatNumber of "3" in a substance administration event means that the current administration is the 3rd in a series.

  5. MAY contain [0..1] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-7473, CONF:1098-7474, CONF:1098-15606)
    1. Contains @typeCode="REFR" REFR
    2. Conforms to Medication Supply Order2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.17:2014-06-09)
  6. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product such that it SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23:2014-06-09) (CONF:1098-7459, CONF:1098-15607)
  7. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product such that it SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54:2014-06-09) (CONF:1098-9331, CONF:1098-31696)
  8. supply act SHALL contain one product/Medication Information (V2) or one product/Immunization Medication Information (V2) template (CONF:1098-9333)
  1. SHALL conform to Medication Dispense template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.18)
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="SPLY" (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 HL7ActClass) (CONF:7451, R2.0=CONF:1098-7451)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode="EVN" Event (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1001 HL7ActMood) (CONF:7452, R2.0=CONF:1098-7452)
  4. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (Prescription Number) (CONF:7454, R2.0=CONF:1098-7454)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-7455), which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Medication Fill Status 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.12.80.64 STATIC 2014-04-23 (CONF:1098-32361)
  6. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (Dispense Date) (CONF:7456, R2.0=CONF:1098-7456)
  7. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] repeatNumber (CONF:1098-7457)

    In "INT" (intent) mood, the repeatNumber defines the number of allowed administrations. For example, a repeatNumber of "3" means that the substance can be administered up to 3 times. In "EVN" (event) mood, the repeatNumber is the number of occurrences. For example, a repeatNumber of "3" in a substance administration event means that the current administration is the 3rd in a series.

  8. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] quantity (Quantity Dispensed) (CONF:7458, R2.0=CONF:1098-7458)
  9. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] performer such that it (CONF:7461, R2.0=CONF:1098-7461)
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedEntity such that it (CONF:7467, R2.0=CONF:1098-7467)
      1. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] addr (CONF:7468, R2.0=CONF:1098-7468)
  10. MAY contain [0..1] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-7473, CONF:1098-7474, CONF:1098-15606)
    1. Contains @typeCode="REFR" REFR
    2. Conforms to Medication Supply Order2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.17:2014-06-09)
  11. supply act SHALL contain one product/Medication Information or one product/Immunization Medication Information template (CONF:15607, CONF:15608, CONF:9333)
  12. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product such that it SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23:2014-06-09) (CONF:1098-7459, CONF:1098-15607)
  13. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product such that it SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54:2014-06-09) (CONF:1098-9331, CONF:1098-31696)
  14. supply act SHALL contain one product/Medication Information (V2) or one product/Immunization Medication Information (V2) template (CONF:1098-9333)
