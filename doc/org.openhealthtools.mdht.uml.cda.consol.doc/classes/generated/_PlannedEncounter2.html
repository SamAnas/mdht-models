Planned Encounter2 - conformance rules

[Encounter: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.40]

This template represents a planned or ordered encounter. The type of encounter (e.g., comprehensive outpatient visit) is represented. Clinicians participating in the encounter and the location of the planned encounter may be captured. The priority that the patient and providers place on the encounter may be represented.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-30437, CONF:1098-30438, CONF:1098-8567 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.40"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09"
  2. SHALL conform to Plan Of Care Activity Encounter template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.40)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Planned moodCode (Act/Encounter/Procedure) 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.23 STATIC 2014-09-01 (CONF:1098-8565, DSTU:1047)
  4. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] code, which SHOULD be selected from ValueSet Encounter Planned 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.52 DYNAMIC (CONF:1098-31032)

    Records the type of encounter ordered or recommended.

  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-30439)/@code="active" Active (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.14 ActStatus) (CONF:1098-31880)
  6. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-30440)
  7. MAY contain zero or more [0..*] performer such that it (CONF:1098-30442)

    Performers represent clinicians who are responsible for assessing and treating the patient.

    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedEntity, where its type is CDA Assigned Entity (CONF:1098-31874)
  8. SHOULD contain [0..*] author such that it (CONF:1098-32045)
    1. Conforms to Author Participation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119)
  9. MAY contain [0..*] participant such that it (CONF:1098-30443, CONF:1098-31876)

    1. This location participation captures where the planned or ordered encounter may take place.

    2. Conforms to Service Delivery Location (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.32)
  10. MAY contain [0..1] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31033, CONF:1098-31034, CONF:1098-31035)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the priority that a patient or a provider places on the encounter.

    2. Contains @typeCode="REFR" REFR
    3. Conforms to Priority Preference (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143)
  11. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31877, CONF:1098-31878, CONF:1098-31879)

    1. The following entryRelationship captures the reason for the planned or ordered encounter

    2. Contains @typeCode="RSON" RSON
    3. Conforms to Indication2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.19:2014-06-09)
  12. participant with target entry serviceDeliveryLocation if present, SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="LOC" Location (CodeSystem: HL7ActRelationshipType 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1002) (CONF:1098-31875)
  1. SHALL conform to Plan Of Care Activity Encounter template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.40)
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="ENC" (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 HL7ActClass) (CONF:8564, R2.0=CONF:1098-8564)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Planned moodCode (Act/Encounter/Procedure) 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.23 STATIC 2014-09-01 (CONF:1098-8565, DSTU:1047)
