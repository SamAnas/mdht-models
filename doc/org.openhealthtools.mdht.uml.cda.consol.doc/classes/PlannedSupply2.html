Planned Supply2

[Supply: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.43]

This template represents both medicinal and non-medicinal supplies ordered, requested, or intended for the patient (e.g., medication prescription, order for wheelchair). The importance of the supply order or request to the patient and provider may be indicated in the Priority Preference. The effective time indicates the time when the supply is intended to take place and author time indicates when the documentation of the plan occurred. The Planned Supply template may also indicate the potential insurance coverage for the procedure. Depending on the type of supply, the product or participant will be either a Medication Information product (medication), an Immunization Medication Information product (immunization), or a Product Instance participant (device/equipment).

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-30463, CONF:1098-30464, CONF:1098-32556 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.43"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09"
  2. SHALL conform to Plan Of Care Activity Supply template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.43)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Planned moodCode (SubstanceAdministration/Supply) 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.24 STATIC 2011-09-30 (CONF:1098-8578)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-30458)/@code="active" Active (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.14 ActStatus) (CONF:1098-32047)
  5. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-30459)

    The effectiveTime in a planned supply represents the time that the supply should occur.

  6. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] repeatNumber (CONF:1098-32063)

    In a Planned Supply, repeatNumber indicates the number of times the supply event can occur. For example, if a medication is filled at a pharmacy and the prescription may be refilled 3 more times, the supply RepeatNumber equals 4.

  7. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] quantity (CONF:1098-32064)
  8. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product such that it (CONF:1098-32049, CONF:1098-32051)

    This product represents medication that is ordered, requested or intended for the patient or this product represents immunization medication that is ordered, requested or intended for the patient.

    1. MAY contain exactly one [1..1] manufacturedProduct such that it
      1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23:2014-06-09) or SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54:2014-06-09) (CONF:1098-32050, CONF:1098-32052)
  9. SHOULD contain [0..1] product such that it (CONF:1098-32325)

    A product is recommended or even required under certain implementations. This IG makes product as recommended (SHOULD).

    1. Conforms to Product from CDA
  10. MAY contain [0..*] performer such that it (CONF:1098-32048)

    1. The clinician who is expected to perform the supply could be identified using supply/performer.

    2. Conforms to Performer2 from CDA
  11. SHOULD contain [0..1] author such that it (CONF:1098-31129)

    1. The author in a supply represents the clinician who is requesting or planning the supply.

    2. Conforms to Author Participation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119)
  12. MAY contain [0..1] participant such that it (CONF:1098-32094, CONF:1098-32095)

    1. This participant represents a device that is ordered, requested or intended for the patient.

    2. Conforms to Product Instance (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.37)
  13. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31110, CONF:1098-31111, CONF:1098-31112)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the priority that a patient or a provider places on the supply.

    2. Contains @typeCode="REFR" REFR
    3. Conforms to Priority Preference (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143)
  14. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-32054, CONF:1098-32055, CONF:1098-32056)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the indication for the supply.

    2. Contains @typeCode="RSON" RSON
    3. Conforms to Indication2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.19:2014-06-09)
  15. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-32057, CONF:1098-32058, CONF:1098-32059)

    1. The following entryRelationship captures any instructions associated with the planned supply.

    2. Contains @typeCode="SUBJ" SUBJ
    3. Conforms to Instruction2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.20:2014-06-09)
  16. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-32060, CONF:1098-32061, CONF:1098-32062)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the insurance coverage the patient may have for the supply.

    2. Contains @typeCode="COMP" COMP
    3. Conforms to Planned Coverage (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.129)
  17. If the product is Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23:2014-06-09) then the product SHALL NOT be Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54:2014-06-09) and the participant SHALL NOT be Product Instance. If the product is Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54:2014-06-09) then the product SHALL NOT be Medication Information (V2) (identifier: urn:hl7ii:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23:2014-06-09) and the participant SHALL NOT be Product Instance. If the participant is Product Instance then the product SHALL NOT be Medication Information (V2) and the product SHALL NOT be Immunization Medication Information (V2). (CONF:1098-32092, CONF:1098-32093, CONF:1098-32096)
