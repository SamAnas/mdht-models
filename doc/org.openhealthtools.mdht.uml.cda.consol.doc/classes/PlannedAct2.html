Planned Act2

[Act: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.39]

This template represents planned acts that are not classified as an observation or a procedure according to the HL7 RIM. Examples of these acts are a dressing change, the teaching or feeding of a patient or the providing of comfort measures. The priority of the activity to the patient and provider is communicated through Priority Preference. The effectiveTime indicates the time when the activity is intended to take place.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-30430, CONF:1098-30431, CONF:1098-32552 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.39"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09"
  2. SHALL conform to Plan Of Care Activity Act template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.39)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Planned moodCode (Act/Encounter/Procedure) 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.23 STATIC 2011-09-30 (CONF:1098-8539)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1098-31687)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-30432)/@code="active" Active (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.14 ActStatus) (CONF:1098-32019)
  6. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-30433)

    The effectiveTime in a planned act represents the time that the act should occur.

  7. MAY contain [0..*] performer such that it (CONF:1098-30435)

    1. The clinician who is expected to carry out the act could be identified using act/performer.

    2. Conforms to Performer2 from CDA
  8. SHOULD contain [0..1] author such that it (CONF:1098-32020)

    1. The author in a planned act represents the clinician who is requesting or planning the act.

    2. Conforms to Author Participation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119)
  9. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31067, CONF:1098-31068, CONF:1098-31069)
    1. Contains @typeCode="REFR" REFR
    2. Conforms to Priority Preference (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143)
  10. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-32021, CONF:1098-32022, CONF:1098-32023)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the indication for the act.

    2. Contains @typeCode="RSON" RSON
    3. Conforms to Indication2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.19:2014-06-09)
  11. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-32024, CONF:1098-32025, CONF:1098-32026)

    1. The following entryRelationship captures any instructions associated with the planned act.

    2. Contains @typeCode="SUBJ" SUBJ
    3. Conforms to Instruction2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.20:2014-06-09)
  12. The code in a Planned Act SHOULD be selected from LOINC (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1) or SNOMED CT (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.96) (CONF:1098-32030)
