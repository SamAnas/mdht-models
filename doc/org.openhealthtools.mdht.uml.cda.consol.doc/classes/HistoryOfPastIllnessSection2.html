History of Past Illness Section (V3)

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.20]

This section contains a record of the patient's past complaints, problems, and diagnoses. It contains data from the patient's past up to the patient's current complaint or reason for seeking medical care.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1198-7828, CONF:1198-10390, CONF:1198-32536 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.20"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2015-08-01"
  2. SHALL conform to History Of Past Illness Section template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.20)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1198-15474)/@code="11348-0" History of Past Illness (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC) (CONF:1198-15475, CONF:1198-30831)
  4. If section/@nullFlavor is not present, MAY contain [0..*] entry such that it (CONF:1198-8791, CONF:1198-15476)
    1. Conforms to Problem Observation2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.4:2015-08-01)
