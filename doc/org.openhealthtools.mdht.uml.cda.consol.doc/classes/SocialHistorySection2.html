Social History Section (V3)

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.17]

This section contains social history data that influence a patient's physical, psychological or emotional health (e.g., smoking status, pregnancy). Demographic data, such as marital status, race, ethnicity, and religious affiliation, is captured in the header.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1198-7936, CONF:1198-10449, CONF:1198-32494 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.17"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2015-08-01"
  2. SHALL conform to Social History Section template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.17)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1198-14819)/@code="29762-2" Social History (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC) (CONF:1198-14820, CONF:1198-30814)
