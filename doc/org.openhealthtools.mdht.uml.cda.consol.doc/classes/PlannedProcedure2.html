Planned Procedure2

[Procedure: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.41]

This template represents planned alterations of the patient's physical condition. Examples of such procedures are tracheostomy, knee replacement, and craniectomy. The priority of the procedure to the patient and provider is communicated through Priority Preference. The effectiveTime indicates the time when the procedure is intended to take place and authorTime indicates when the documentation of the plan occurred. The Planned Procedure Template may also indicate the potential insurance coverage for the procedure.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-30444, CONF:1098-30445, CONF:1098-32554 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.41"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09"
  2. SHALL conform to Plan Of Care Activity Procedure template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.41)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Planned moodCode (Act/Encounter/Procedure) 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.23 STATIC 2011-09-30 (CONF:1098-8569)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1098-31976)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-30446)/@code="active" Active (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.14 ActStatus) (CONF:1098-31978)
  6. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-30447)

    The effectiveTime in a planned procedure represents the time that the procedure should occur.

  7. MAY contain zero or more [0..*] methodCode (CONF:1098-31980)

    In a planned procedure the provider may suggest that a procedure should be performed using a particular method. MethodCode SHALL NOT conflict with the method inherent in Procedure / code.

  8. MAY contain zero or more [0..*] targetSiteCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Body Site 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.12.3221.8.9 DYNAMIC (CONF:1098-31981)

    The targetSiteCode is used to identify the part of the body of concern for the planned procedure.

  9. MAY contain [0..*] performer such that it (CONF:1098-30449)

    1. The clinician who is expected to perform the procedure could be identified using procedure/performer.

    2. Conforms to Performer2 from CDA
  10. SHOULD contain [0..1] author such that it (CONF:1098-31979)

    1. The author in a planned procedure represents the clinician who is requesting or planning the procedure.

    2. Conforms to Author Participation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119)
  11. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31079, CONF:1098-31080, CONF:1098-31081)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the priority that a patient or a provider places on the procedure.

    2. Contains @typeCode="REFR" REFR
    3. Conforms to Priority Preference (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143)
  12. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31982, CONF:1098-31983, CONF:1098-31984)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the indication for the procedure.

    2. Contains @typeCode="RSON" RSON
    3. Conforms to Indication2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.19:2014-06-09)
  13. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31985, CONF:1098-31986, CONF:1098-31989)

    1. The following entryRelationship captures any instructions associated with the planned procedure.

    2. Contains @typeCode="SUBJ" SUBJ
    3. Conforms to Instruction2 (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.20:2014-06-09)
  14. MAY contain [0..*] entryRelationship such that it (CONF:1098-31990, CONF:1098-31991, CONF:1098-31992)

    1. The following entryRelationship represents the insurance coverage the patient may have for the procedure.

    2. Contains @typeCode="COMP" COMP
    3. Conforms to Planned Coverage (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.129)
  15. The procedure/code in a planned procedure SHOULD be selected from LOINC (codeSystem 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1) or SNOMED CT (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.96) (CONF:1098-31977)
  16. The procedure/code in a planned procedure MAY be selected from CPT-4 (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.12) or ICD10 PCS (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.4) (CONF:1098-31977)
  17. entryRelationship with target entry Instruction2 if present, SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @inversionInd="true" True (CONF:1098-31987)
