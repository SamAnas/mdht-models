Goals Section

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.60]

This template represents patient Goals. A goal is a defined outcome or condition to be achieved in the process of patient care. Goals include patient-defined over-arching goals (e.g., alleviation of health concerns, desired/intended positive outcomes from interventions, longevity, function, symptom management, comfort) and health concern-specific or intervention-specific goals to achieve desired outcomes.

    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.60"
  2. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] @nullFlavor="NI" (CONF:1098-32819)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1098-29586)/@code="61146-7" Goals (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC) (CONF:1098-29588)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] title (CONF:1098-30721)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] text (CONF:1098-30722)
  6. If section/@nullFlavor is not present, SHALL contain [1..*] entry such that it (CONF:1098-30719, CONF:1098-30720)
