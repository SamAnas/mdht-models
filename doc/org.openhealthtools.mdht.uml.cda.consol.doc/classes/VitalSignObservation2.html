Vital Sign Observation2

[Observation: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.27]

This template represents measurement of common vital signs. Vital signs are represented with additional vocabulary constraints for type of vital sign and unit of measure.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1098-7299, CONF:1098-10527, CONF:1098-32574 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.27"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09"
  2. SHALL conform to Vital Sign Observation template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.27)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-7304)

    null

  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] value with @xsi:type="PQ" (CONF:1098-7305)
  5. contain zero or one [0..1] text
  6. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] interpretationCode (CONF:1098-7307), which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Observation Interpretation (HL7) DYNAMIC 2.16.840.1.113883.1.11.78 DYNAMIC (CONF:1098-32886, DSTU:1220)
  7. SHOULD contain [0..*] author such that it (CONF:1098-7310)
    1. Conforms to Author Participation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119)
  8. contain [0..1] author such that it
    1. Conforms to Author from CDA
  9. MAY satisfy: This constraint is being overridden as it has been removed in v2 and should never fire
  10. MAY satisfy: This constraint is being overridden as it has been removed in v2 and should never fire
  11. MAY satisfy: This constraint is being overridden as it has been removed in v2 and should never fire
