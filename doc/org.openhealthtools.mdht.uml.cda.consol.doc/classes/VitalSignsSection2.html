Vital Signs Section (entries required) (V3)

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.4.1]

The Vital Signs Section contains relevant vital signs for the context and use case of the document type, such as blood pressure, heart rate, respiratory rate, height, weight, body mass index, head circumference, pulse oximetry, temperature, and body surface area. The section should include notable vital signs such as the most recent, maximum and/or minimum, baseline, or relevant trends. Vital signs are represented in the same way as other results, but are aggregated into their own section to follow clinical conventions.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( CONF:1198-7273, CONF:1198-10452, CONF:1198-32585 ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.4.1"
    2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2015-08-01"
  2. SHALL conform to Vital Signs Section Entries Optional2 template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.4:2015-08-01)
  3. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] @nullFlavor="NI" (CONF:1198-32874)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1198-15962)/@code="8716-3" Vital Signs (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC) (CONF:1198-15963, CONF:1198-30903)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] title (CONF:1198-9967)
  6. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] text (CONF:1198-7275)
  7. If section/@nullFlavor is not present, SHALL contain [1..*] entry such that it (CONF:1198-7276, CONF:1198-15964)

    If section/@nullFlavor is not present:
